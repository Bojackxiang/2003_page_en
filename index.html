About AISF

AISF is short for Australia International Students Festival. It is the first large-scale festival focusing on international students’activities and experience in Australia. Unlike the traditional education exhibitions where students can only interact with representatives from universities or other education providers, the AISF committee has upgraded it to a new level.
The Committee is aiming to build a comprehensive platform which provides international students varieties of quality service and information from education institutes as well as companies from different industries, achieving a one-stop platform for information access to all they need while studying in Australia.

ALL PASS

The Allpass Discount Card is specially launched by the AISF Committee, offering a variety of special offers and discounts exclusive to international students in dining, entertainment, shopping and other services. The Discount Card will be valid for a year. Meanwhile, the Committee will be endeavour to increase the number of collaborative shops, restaurants and other service providers as well as the amount of discounts.
